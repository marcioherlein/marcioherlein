
# Marcio Herlein — Portfolio Website

## Overview
A sleek, Apple-inspired single-page portfolio with scroll-triggered animations, muted tones, large typography, and immersive parallax effects. The design will use a light/neutral color palette with smooth reveal animations as you scroll — similar to Apple's product pages.

---

## Sections

### 1. Hero Section
- Full-viewport landing with your name in bold oversized typography
- Subtitle: "Finance × Technology" tagline
- Subtle parallax background with a gradient or abstract financial motif
- Smooth fade-in and scale animation on load
- Scroll indicator at the bottom

### 2. About Me
- Text and photo section that reveals as you scroll into view
- Brief bio highlighting your blend of financial analysis and software development
- Words/stats that animate in (e.g., "Years of Experience", "Projects Delivered")
- Clean, editorial layout with generous whitespace

### 3. Projects — Case Studies (Scroll-Animated)
Each project gets its own full-screen scroll-driven section with rich animations:

**Project 1: VAL-X — Valuation Dashboard**
- Screenshot/mockup that scales and pins as you scroll
- Text slides in describing the project: what it does, your role, the tech stack
- Key features animate in sequentially (live market data, DCF models, equity coverage)
- Link to live site

**Project 2: CDP — Creative Director Portfolio**
- Similar scroll-pinned showcase with screenshot
- Description of the creative challenge and how you built it
- Highlight of scroll animations and visual storytelling techniques used
- Link to live site

### 4. Testimonials
- A horizontally scrolling or fade-through carousel of quotes
- Each testimonial card animates in on scroll
- Clean typography with subtle accent colors

### 5. Contact Section
- Minimalist contact form or direct links (email, LinkedIn, GitHub)
- Smooth fade-in as you reach the bottom
- Call-to-action: "Let's build something together"

---

## Scroll & Animation Features (Apple-Style)
- **Scroll-triggered reveals**: Elements fade and slide up as they enter the viewport
- **Sticky/pinned sections**: Project showcases pin in place while content scrolls over them
- **Parallax layers**: Subtle depth effect on hero and transitions between sections
- **Text animations**: Headlines that reveal word-by-word or character-by-character
- **Smooth transitions**: Sections blend into each other with opacity and scale changes
- **Progress indicator**: A subtle scroll progress bar at the top

---

## Navigation
- Fixed top navbar that appears after scrolling past the hero
- Smooth scroll links to each section
- Minimal and translucent, staying out of the way

## Design Language
- **Palette**: Light backgrounds (off-white/warm gray), dark text, one muted accent color
- **Typography**: Large, bold headings; clean sans-serif body text
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace, breathing room between sections
- **Mobile responsive**: All animations gracefully adapt to smaller screens
